# Reactivate cancelled subscription

## Summary

Cancelled subscriptions may need a new subscription rather than direct reactivation, depending on backend support and policy.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Review cancelled subscription and cancellation date.
3. Check vehicle and payment state.
4. If reactivation is supported, reactivate and confirm plan and vehicle.
5. If not supported, direct customer to create a new membership or approved CSR creation flow.
6. Add support note.

## Portal fields to check

- Cancelled subscription
- Cancellation audit event
- Vehicle record
- Payment method status
- Desired plan

## Escalate if

- Customer disputes cancellation.
- Customer expects previous price or promotion.
- Reactivation creates duplicate billing risk.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can review the cancelled membership. Depending on policy, we may be able to reactivate it, or we may need to start a new membership for the vehicle.
