# Update account contact information

## Summary

CSRs can update customer account information such as name, email, and phone number when identity is verified.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm exact fields to update.
3. Update name, email, phone, or other supported fields.
4. Read back updated values for confirmation.
5. Add support note if the update was part of a larger issue.
6. Confirm audit timeline records account update.

## Portal fields to check

- Customer first and last name
- Email
- Phone
- Status
- Audit timeline

## Escalate if

- Customer cannot verify identity.
- Email or phone is already tied to another account.
- Customer requests account merge or deletion.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can update your account contact information after verifying the account. This helps future receipts, login links, and support lookups go to the correct contact details.
