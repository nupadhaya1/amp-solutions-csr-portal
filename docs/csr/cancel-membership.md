# Cancel membership

## Summary

Cancelling a membership stops future membership billing for the selected subscription. It does not necessarily delete the customer account.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm which subscription and vehicle the customer wants to cancel.
3. Explain that future billing stops and the covered vehicle loses membership access.
4. Review overdue balance, refund policy, and effective date if modeled.
5. Use the cancel subscription action.
6. Add cancellation reason in support notes.
7. Confirm audit timeline records the cancellation.

## Portal fields to check

- Active subscriptions
- Vehicle tied to subscription
- Next billing date
- Latest payment status
- Purchase history
- Audit timeline

## Escalate if

- Customer requests refund outside normal policy.
- Customer disputes prior charges.
- Cancellation fails due to backend error.
- Customer requests account deletion or privacy action.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can cancel the selected membership so it stops future billing and the vehicle will no longer have membership wash access. Account history can remain unless you request a separate deletion process.
