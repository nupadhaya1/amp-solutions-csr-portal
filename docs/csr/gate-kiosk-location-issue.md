# Gate, kiosk, or location issue

## Summary

If the account and subscription are valid but the customer still cannot wash, the issue may be operational rather than account-based.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Confirm customer account and subscription are active.
2. Confirm vehicle is covered and plate is correct.
3. Ask which location and approximate time.
4. Review purchase history for kiosk or single wash transaction if applicable.
5. Add detailed support note.
6. Escalate to location operations if account appears valid.
7. If charged without service, follow refund or duplicate charge policy.

## Portal fields to check

- Customer status
- Subscription status
- Vehicle plate
- Purchase history
- Location metadata if available
- Support notes from related cases

## Escalate if

- Multiple customers are affected.
- Gate, kiosk, tunnel, or location is down.
- Customer reports safety or damage issue.
- Account is active but access is denied.

## Customer-safe explanation

Your account looks valid, so this may be a location or equipment issue. I will document the location and time and escalate it for review.
