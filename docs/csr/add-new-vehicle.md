# Add a new vehicle

## Summary

Adding a vehicle creates a vehicle record but does not always grant subscription coverage. Coverage depends on plan and subscription assignment.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Collect vehicle year, make, model, license plate, and state.
3. Normalize license plate before saving.
4. Check whether the plate exists on another account.
5. Add the vehicle.
6. If the customer expects membership coverage, assign or transfer coverage through the correct subscription workflow.
7. Add a support note if the vehicle add was connected to a support issue.

## Portal fields to check

- Vehicle list
- License plate
- Plate state
- Existing subscriptions
- Plan capacity

## Escalate if

- Plate already exists under another customer.
- Customer cannot provide enough vehicle information.
- Vehicle add action fails.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can add the vehicle to your account. Adding the vehicle records it in your profile, but membership wash access depends on whether a subscription is assigned to that vehicle.
