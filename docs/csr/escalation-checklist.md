# Escalation checklist

## Summary

Escalate when the CSR cannot safely or accurately resolve the issue within normal tooling or policy.

## Escalate billing issues when

- Duplicate successful charges are visible.
- Refund exceeds CSR policy.
- Chargeback, fraud, or dispute language is used.
- Bank statement shows charge but portal has no transaction.
- Payment processor and portal disagree.

## Escalate account issues when

- Duplicate accounts need merge.
- Identity is unclear.
- Customer requests deletion or privacy action.
- Vehicle belongs to another account.

## Escalate operations issues when

- Gate, kiosk, tunnel, or location issue affects an active customer.
- Multiple customers are impacted.
- Damage or safety complaint occurs.
- Location outage is suspected.

## Minimum escalation note

Include customer name and contact, vehicle or plate, subscription status, payment details, location and time, what the customer said, what the CSR checked, action taken, and requested next step.

## Customer-safe explanation

I am going to document everything we checked and escalate this to the right team so they can review the issue with the needed context.
