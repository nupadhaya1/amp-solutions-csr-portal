# Change license plate

## Summary

Incorrect plate information can cause gate denial even when the subscription is active.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm the affected vehicle.
3. Confirm new license plate and state.
4. Normalize plate format.
5. Update vehicle record if supported.
6. Add support note explaining the plate correction.
7. Escalate to location or plate reader review if access still fails.

## Portal fields to check

- Vehicle license plate
- Plate state
- Subscription status
- Recent gate or location notes

## Escalate if

- Plate belongs to another account.
- Temporary plate cannot be recognized.
- Plate reader failure persists.

## Customer-safe explanation

Your membership may be active, but the gate uses vehicle and plate information to identify coverage. I will update the plate on the vehicle record and document the change.
