# Damage or safety complaint

## Summary

Damage and safety complaints should be documented and escalated immediately according to policy.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Stay neutral and empathetic.
2. Collect account, vehicle, location, date, and time.
3. Ask for a concise description.
4. Do not admit liability.
5. Add detailed support note.
6. Escalate to supervisor or location operations immediately.
7. Provide next-step expectations if policy allows.

## Portal fields to check

- Customer contact details
- Vehicle details
- Purchase or location history
- Support notes
- Audit timeline

## Escalate if

- Always escalate damage or safety complaints.
- Injury, police, insurance, or legal language is mentioned.
- Customer requests compensation.

## Customer-safe explanation

I am sorry that happened. I will document the location, time, vehicle, and description, then escalate it to the appropriate team for review. I cannot determine liability from this support call.
