# Duplicate customer account

## Summary

Duplicate accounts can cause missing purchases, wrong vehicle coverage, login confusion, and billing confusion.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Search by phone, email, name, and license plate.
2. Compare candidate accounts carefully.
3. Determine which account owns the active subscription, purchase, or vehicle.
4. Do not merge or delete accounts unless the portal has a supported workflow.
5. Add support notes to affected accounts if needed.
6. Escalate for merge if policy or tooling requires it.

## Portal fields to check

- Customer contact details
- Vehicle plates
- Purchase history
- Active subscriptions
- Audit timelines

## Escalate if

- Both accounts have active billing.
- Purchases need migration.
- Vehicle is attached to the wrong account.
- Identity ownership is unclear.

## Customer-safe explanation

I found more than one possible account. I need to confirm which one has the active membership or purchase so we do not change the wrong account.
