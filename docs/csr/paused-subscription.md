# Paused subscription

## Summary

A paused subscription may block washes until it is resumed, depending on AMP policy.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm subscription status is `PAUSED`.
3. Explain current access and billing impact.
4. If customer wants to resume and action exists, resume subscription.
5. Confirm next billing date and status after resume.
6. Add support note.

## Portal fields to check

- Subscription status
- Pause date or reason if available
- Next billing date
- Covered vehicle

## Escalate if

- Resume action is unavailable.
- Customer was paused unintentionally.
- Billing date after resume is disputed.

## Customer-safe explanation

Your subscription is currently paused, so membership access may not be available. I can review whether it can be resumed and confirm the billing and access impact.
