# Transfer subscription to a new vehicle

## Summary

Customers who buy a new vehicle may need active subscription coverage moved from the old vehicle to the new vehicle.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Confirm old vehicle currently covered by the subscription.
3. Collect new vehicle year, make, model, plate, and state.
4. Add the new vehicle if it does not exist.
5. Use transfer coverage from old vehicle to new vehicle.
6. Confirm old vehicle no longer has coverage.
7. Confirm new vehicle shows active coverage.
8. Add a support note and verify audit events.

## Portal fields to check

- Old vehicle
- New vehicle
- Subscription vehicle assignment
- Subscription status
- Plan capacity
- Audit event for transfer

## Escalate if

- New plate belongs to another account.
- Transfer action fails.
- Customer wants multiple vehicles covered but plan supports one.
- Subscription is overdue or cancelled.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can move membership coverage from your old vehicle to the new vehicle. Once complete, the old vehicle will no longer be covered and the new one should be recognized for membership washes.
