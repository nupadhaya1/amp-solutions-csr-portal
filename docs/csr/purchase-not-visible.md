# Purchase not visible in account

## Summary

A purchase may be missing because the customer used a different email or phone, bought at a kiosk without account association, or has duplicate accounts.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Search by phone, email, name, and license plate.
2. Ask for approximate purchase date, amount, and location.
3. Check duplicate customer accounts.
4. Review purchase histories across matching accounts.
5. If purchase remains missing, document details and escalate to billing or operations.

## Portal fields to check

- Purchase history
- Duplicate accounts
- Vehicle license plate
- Customer contact details

## Escalate if

- Customer has bank charge but no matching portal purchase.
- Purchase may be tied to another customer.
- Refund or receipt is needed but transaction cannot be found.

## Customer-safe explanation

I do not see that purchase on this account yet. I will check whether it was made under another phone or email, or whether it needs billing or location review.
