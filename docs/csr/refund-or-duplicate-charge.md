# Refund or duplicate charge

## Summary

Duplicate charges and refund requests require transaction review and may need escalation depending on policy.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Review purchase history for duplicate date, amount, type, or location.
3. Check whether one transaction failed and one succeeded.
4. Check whether a refund already exists.
5. Document the customer claim in support notes.
6. If policy allows CSR refund, use the approved workflow.
7. Otherwise escalate to billing or supervisor with transaction details.

## Portal fields to check

- Purchase dates
- Purchase amounts
- Purchase statuses
- Purchase type
- Refund status
- Location or kiosk metadata if available

## Escalate if

- Customer threatens chargeback.
- Duplicate charge is not visible in the portal.
- Charge appears tied to another account.
- Refund exceeds normal CSR policy.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can review the purchase history and check whether there are duplicate successful charges, a failed attempt, or an existing refund. If this requires manual billing review, I will document and escalate it.
