# Coupon redemption issue

## Summary

Coupon issues may be caused by expiration, prior redemption, account mismatch, eligibility rules, or kiosk/app errors.

## CSR remediation steps

1. Search for the customer profile.
2. Review purchase history for coupon redemption or discount transaction.
3. Ask for the coupon or promotion name if needed.
4. Check whether the coupon was expired, already redeemed, or not eligible.
5. If the customer paid full price due to coupon failure, determine whether policy allows refund or credit.
6. Add a support note with coupon details and outcome.

## Portal fields to check

- Purchase type and status
- Coupon or discount metadata if available
- Customer account used for redemption
- Related transaction amount

## Escalate if

- Coupon eligibility is unclear.
- Promotion appears broken for multiple customers.
- Customer requests manual credit or refund outside policy.

## Customer-safe explanation

I can check whether the coupon was redeemed, expired, already used, or not eligible for that purchase. If it looks like a system issue, I will document and escalate it.
